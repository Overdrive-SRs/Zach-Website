Amazon Web Services & Alexa

Team Project

As a team of three, we learned about different areas of Amazon Web Services to create an EC2 instance that allowed us to host a website on the internet. The purpose of the website was to show off an IoT idea that we came up with as a group. Our idea was the ability to talk to Alexa to activate various functions of a garden remotely. We connected Alexa to a database which was displayed on the website and updated whenever a new intent was used on the Alexa developer console.
